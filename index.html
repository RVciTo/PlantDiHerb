Pothos

"Couch Creeper Queen"

This trailing beauty is the ultimate low-maintenance companion who loves to cascade dramatically from shelves while secretly judging your Netflix choices. She's the friend who always looks effortlessly gorgeous even when you forget about her for weeks.

Green Routine

  • Water: When the top inch of soil feels dry (usually every 1-2 weeks). She's forgiving if you forget!
  • Light: Bright, indirect light but tolerates lower light like a champ
  • Humidity: Regular household humidity is perfect
  • Temperature: Comfortable room temperature (65-75Â°F)
  • Fertilizer: Monthly during growing season with diluted liquid fertilizer

Boston Fern

"Misty Midnights"

This dramatic diva loves to live her best life in humid, spa-like conditions. She's the plant equivalent of someone who needs their daily mist and gets cranky without proper pampering. Absolutely gorgeous when happy, absolutely dramatic when not.

Green Routine

  • Water: Keep soil consistently moist but never soggy - she's high maintenance like that
  • Light: Bright, indirect light - direct sun will crisp her delicate fronds
  • Humidity: High humidity (50%+) - mist daily or use a humidifier
  • Temperature: Cool to moderate (60-70Â°F) - she doesn't like it too hot
  • Fertilizer: Light feeding monthly during growing season

She's crying out for more humidity! Increase misting, add a pebble tray, or invest in a humidifier. Also check that she's not getting direct sunlight.
